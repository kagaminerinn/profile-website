Techzen.id Webinar 2021

Nov 2021 - Dec 2021

MTDD (Multimedia & Design)

Techzen.id Webinar 2021 is a project carried out by the Department of Information System batch 2021 at President University to create a webinar whose topic is to discuss the importance of emotional intelligence and networking in building careers.

  • Discussions to determine themes and designs that will be used for Techzen.id on the Instagram platform
  • Division of tasks to create designs for opening and closing
  • Create designs with predefined themes using the Canva App
